Available Medical Tests

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

A comprehensive test to evaluate your overall health and detect a wide range of disorders.

Lipid Panel

Measures cholesterol and other fats in your blood to assess cardiovascular risk.

Electrocardiogram (ECG)

Records the electrical signal from the heart to check for different heart conditions.

Thyroid Function Test

Checks how well your thyroid gland is working by measuring hormone levels.

Urinalysis

A test of your urine used to detect and manage a wide range of medical disorders.

Blood Sugar Test

Measures the amount of glucose in your blood, crucial for diabetes monitoring.
